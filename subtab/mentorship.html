CIIC MENTORSHIP

Mentorship Programme, is a new initiative undertaken by CIIC, the Entrepreneurship cell of Techno India which would act like a guide to participant E-cells in various colleges all across India through a planned approach. Colleges participating in Mentorship Programme would be associated with CIIC over a extensive time period and would develop in the process, as a result of collaborative efforts.
